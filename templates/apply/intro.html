Student Application

Welcome to the Girls of Steel application process! We're excited that you're interested in joining our programs.

The application process involves the following steps:

  • Selecting the program you're applying to.
  • Verifying your identity via email.
  • Providing student and parent/guardian information.
  • Answering a few essay questions about your interest.

Once submitted, our lead mentors will review your application. You'll receive an email confirmation shortly after submission.
